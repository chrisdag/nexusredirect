Nexus has moved

If you see this page, you are accessing the old Nexus URL. The Nexus server was moved to the production Openshift VPC in recognition of it's importance to Syngenta operations and CI/CD automation. The secondary reason for the move is that the USAE-2 Openshift environment needed to be emptied of running services in order to support a planned rebuild and upgrade effort.

New Nexus URL: https://nexus.apps.usae-1.syngentaaws.org

New Docker Public Registry URL: https://registry.usae-1.syngentaaws.org

The migration happened in November 2019 but we kept Nexus online here in read-only mode to support a longer transition period.

For Nexus related questions ask a question under the CE project at https://jira.syngentaaws.org or contact Chris Dagdigian (dag@bioteam.net) directly. Thanks!

Thank you for using nginx.
